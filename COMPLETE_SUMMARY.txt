🏗️ ARCHITECTURE SUMMARY:
================================================================================

VISUAL STREAM:
  Raw Video Frames
    ↓
  ResNet50 CNN (pretrained, freezable)
    ↓
  Per-frame Feature Vectors (2048-dim)

KEYPOINT STREAM:
  MediaPipe Hand + Body Landmarks
    ↓
  MLP Encoder (258-dim → 256-dim)
    ↓
  Per-frame Keypoint Vectors

FUSION:
  Concatenate + Project to 256-dim

TEMPORAL MODELING:
  Positional Encoding
    ↓
  Transformer Encoder (4 layers, 8 heads, 256-dim)
    ↓
  Contextual Representations

MODES:

Mode 1: SSL PRETRAINING
  Masked Autoencoder: Mask 75% of frames, reconstruct CNN features
  or
  Contrastive Learning: Learn similar embeddings for augmented views
  → Creates: models/ssl_pretrained.pt

Mode 2: SUPERVISED FINE-TUNING
  Load SSL weights
    ↓
  Mean-pool over time
    ↓
  Classification head (256-dim → vocab_size)
    ↓
  Cross-entropy loss
  → Creates: models/best_model.pt

TOTAL PARAMETERS: ~65M
